About us

You are welcomed by Up – To – Date team!

I'm student of Bauman Moscow State Technical University, group IU7-66B. My site provides the ability to plan tasks, responsibilities of the user. The problem of competent planning is currently relevant.

I, inspired by the technology of S. M. A. R. T., decided to create a simple to use, but at the same time effective scheduler, which includes some of the properties of this famous technology. Up – To – Date scheduler allows you to collect each user task in separate boards and cards, control the frequency of sending notifications.
